Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NPC Labs LLC ("Palate Map," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Palate Map website and services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication credentials (via Google sign-in or email magic link)
  • Taste data: film ratings, scores across 8 categories, quiz responses, and weighting preferences
  • Social data: friend connections and shared taste comparisons
  • Watchlist data: films you save, mark as seen, or queue for later

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, predictions requested, and interactions with the Service
  • Device data: browser type, operating system, and screen size
  • Performance data: Core Web Vitals (page load times, interaction responsiveness) to improve the Service

Information from third parties

  • Google: if you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account
  • TMDB: film metadata (titles, posters, cast, crew) from The Movie Database API

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Generate personalized predictions, analysis, and recommendations
  • Compute your taste profile, palate type, and scoring formula
  • Enable social features (friend connections, taste comparisons)
  • Send transactional emails (magic links, account notifications)
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the product
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions (when applicable)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We share information only with the following service providers, solely to operate the Service:

  • Supabase: database hosting and authentication (US-hosted)
  • Anthropic: AI model provider for predictions and analysis. Your taste data is sent to generate predictions; Anthropic does not retain it for training.
  • Stripe: payment processing (when applicable)
  • Resend: transactional email delivery
  • Google Analytics / GTM: anonymized usage analytics
  • Netlify: website hosting

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States. We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data. All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and export

You can view and export your taste data at any time through the Service.

Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

Correction

You can update your ratings, scores, and profile information at any time within the Service.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Palate Map uses browser local storage to cache your taste profile and preferences for performance. We use Google Analytics cookies for anonymized usage tracking. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify you by email or via a prominent in-app notice. The current version is always available at palatemap.com/privacy.html.
